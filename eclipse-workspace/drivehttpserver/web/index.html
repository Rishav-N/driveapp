Drive Share

This is a Drive Share service. Pick your Ride by choosing "Order a pickup" and purchase a car. We have Suv's, Luxury cars, A standard Car Option, and Minivans. Suv's and Minivans hold 6 - 8 people, Luxury cars and the Standard Option holds 4 - 5 people. After you make your purchase we will tell you the approximate time for your travel, the amount of miles your travel will take and the cost for your purchase. Have fun riding!
